# 12-Week Day 72 - Planning a Compliant Design Response and Evidence Trail

## Purpose

Turn the Day 71 scenario brief into a dependency-ordered design response whose inputs, calculations, decisions, authorised-source checks, interactions and limitations can be reconstructed.

## Prerequisites

- [[12-Week Day 71 - Reading and Decomposing an Integrated Assessment Scenario]]

## Related concepts

- [[Evidence Quality and Applicability]]

## Practical applications

- establishing a design basis;
- recording calculation provenance;
- mapping interacting decisions and reopening triggers;
- preparing an evidence package for inspection and verification review.

## Assessment relevance

The module assesses process quality, traceability and bounded recommendations rather than an unsupported final answer. It does not confer design approval or competency.

## References and review boundary

Exact design criteria, values, clauses and acceptance decisions require current authorised sources and qualified review. The module remains `review-required`, `reference_check_required` and not `technically-reviewed`.
