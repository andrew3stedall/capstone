# 12-Week Day 47 - Rest, Retrieval and Installation-Defect Correction

Day 47 consolidates [[12-Week Day 43 - Wiring-System Selection and Mechanical Protection]], [[12-Week Day 44 - Environmental Influences, Segregation and Support Concepts]], [[12-Week Day 45 - Consumer Mains, Submains and Final Subcircuits]] and [[12-Week Day 46 - Fixed Appliances and Local Isolation Reasoning]] without adding new electrical theory.

## Learning module

- [Day 47 — Rest, Retrieval and Installation-Defect Correction](../learning-plans/12-week/modules/day-47-rest-retrieval-and-installation-defect-correction.md)

## Core workflow

Use **R-E-C-O-V-E-R**: rest first, elicit recall, classify errors, order repairs, verify the smallest source, exercise with a changed scenario and reassess readiness.

## Practical application

The learner completes six closed-note prompts, records confidence, repairs no more than three blocking or high-confidence errors and stops after the defined time box.

## Assessment relevance

A strong response retrieves the Week 7 workflows accurately, identifies the cause of an error, uses bounded source checking, demonstrates transfer and manages fatigue rather than extending catch-up indefinitely.

## Safety boundary

This note authorises no practical switching, testing, isolation, installation or verification. Readiness for the next study block is not evidence of field competence.
