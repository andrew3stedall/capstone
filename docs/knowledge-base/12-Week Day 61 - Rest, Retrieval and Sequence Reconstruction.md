# 12-Week Day 61 - Rest, Retrieval and Sequence Reconstruction

## Purpose

A bounded recovery block for reconstructing Week 9 verification-planning dependencies without adding new electrical theory.

## Core model

Use **R-E-B-U-I-L-D**: Recover first; Elicit from memory; Build the dependency chain; Uncover confidence errors; Isolate up to three repairs; Link repairs to changed scenarios; Decide readiness and stop.

## Prerequisites

- [[12-Week Day 59 - Test Purposes, Dependencies and Safe Sequencing Concepts]]
- [[12-Week Day 60 - Instrument Suitability, Limitations and Pre-Use Evidence]]
- [[Retrieval Practice]]
- [[Confidence Calibration]]

## Practical application

Use a 20–30 minute maximum, closed-note retrieval, one shuffled dependency chain, confidence ratings, no more than three targeted repairs and one changed-context question per repair.

## Assessment relevance

Readiness requires independent recall, reasoned dependency links, prioritised remediation, changed-context transfer and a documented stop decision.

## Safety and review boundary

This note authorises no practical electrical activity. Stop for fatigue, repeated guessing, frustration, time-limit breach or a blocking misconception about sources, authority, operating state or evidence limits.
