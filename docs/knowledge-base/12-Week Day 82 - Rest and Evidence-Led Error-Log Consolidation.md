# 12-Week Day 82 - Rest and Evidence-Led Error-Log Consolidation

## Purpose

Use a short recovery block to classify error mechanisms, repair a maximum of three priority errors and prepare a bounded readiness note without adding new theory.

## Prerequisites

- [[12-Week Day 81 - Staged Inspection, Verification and Fault-Reasoning Mock Assessment]]
- [[Error Log]]
- [[Confidence Calibration]]
- [[Fatigue Management]]

## Related concepts

- [[High-Confidence Errors]]
- [[Dependency Errors]]
- [[Spaced Retrieval]]
- [[Changed-Context Practice]]
- [[Stop Conditions]]

## Practical applications

- prioritising safety-critical and dependency errors;
- repairing reasoning mechanisms rather than answers;
- checking transfer with varied scenarios;
- limiting remediation workload;
- recording readiness and remaining risk.

## Assessment relevance

This block supports recovery and evidence-led preparation for the full mock. It does not add assessment criteria or claim competence.

## References and review boundary

Technical repairs must use authorised evidence. Fatigue or unresolved safety-critical misconceptions require rest or qualified support rather than forced progression.
