# 12-Week Day 81 - Staged Inspection, Verification and Fault-Reasoning Mock Assessment

## Purpose

Integrate fictional inspection observations, verification records and staged fault evidence while preserving traceability, competing hypotheses and bounded conclusions.

## Prerequisites

- [[12-Week Day 80 - Staged Design and Calculation Mock Assessment]]
- [[Visual Inspection]]
- [[Verification Evidence]]
- [[Hypothesis Control]]

## Related concepts

- [[Operating State]]
- [[Evidence Ledger]]
- [[Contradiction Register]]
- [[Re-Verification Planning]]
- [[Technical Review]]

## Practical applications

- separating observations from interpretations;
- mapping verification records to purpose and state;
- ranking competing fault hypotheses;
- updating confidence after contradictory evidence;
- identifying change-driven re-verification triggers.

## Assessment relevance

The original staged mock tests evidence reasoning under time pressure without providing field procedures, official questions or a competency decision.

## References and review boundary

Exact inspection, verification, diagnostic and acceptance requirements require current authorised sources and qualified review. The module authorises no practical work.
