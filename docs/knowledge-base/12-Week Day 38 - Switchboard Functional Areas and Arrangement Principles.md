# 12-Week Day 38 - Switchboard Functional Areas and Arrangement Principles

## Purpose

Builds a functional map of a fictional switchboard before any physical-arrangement or compliance claim is considered.

## Module

## Core concepts

Functional area; arrangement; distribution point; segregation; prospective change; evidence boundary.

## Workflow

**B-O-A-R-D-S** bounds the evidence, outlines sources, assigns functions, relates component groups, detects interactions and states only supported conclusions.

## Practical application and assessment relevance

Learners annotate original board schematics, build evidence tables and explain what reopens after a proposed circuit addition. This develops structured Capstone reasoning without reproducing an official task.

## Safety boundary

Paper-based mapping only. No cover removal, device operation, live access, measurement, construction approval or compliance determination is authorised.
