# Block 37 - Structured Visual Inspection

## Core idea
A structured inspection separates scope, direct observation, limitations, contradictions and follow-up evidence. Appearance alone does not establish hidden condition, cause, compliance or defect severity.

## Retrieval prompts
- What makes an observation neutral and bounded?
- Which six lenses organise the fictional inspection exercise?
- How should inaccessible or contradictory evidence be recorded?

## Safety boundary
This note does not authorise field access, opening equipment or inspecting near exposed live parts. Actual inspection requirements and controls need current authorised sources and competent review.
