# Block 63 - Completion Reflection and Technical-Review Boundary

## Recall prompt
Can I describe what the program demonstrates without overstating competence, compliance or technical approval?

## Completion chain
1. Inventory completed artifacts
2. Compare outcomes with evidence
3. Identify persistent weaknesses
4. Separate learning confidence from technical certainty
5. Collect every review flag
6. Build a qualified-review handoff
7. Record one next-step commitment
8. State the approval boundary

Content completion means the planned educational material exists. It does not mean the material or learner has received qualified technical approval.
