# Block 46 - Documentation and Traceability

## Key idea
A defensible record keeps scope, provenance, observation, interpretation, uncertainty, decision and follow-up linked but distinct.

## Retrieval prompts
- What seven links form the traceability chain?
- Why must operating state be recorded with evidence?
- How does an audit trail differ from silently replacing a conclusion?

## Application cue
When reviewing a record, ask: can another competent person locate the evidence, understand its scope and reconstruct the decision without relying on hidden assumptions?

## Safety boundary
Documentation alone does not establish safety or compliance. Required fields, retention and responsibilities need current authorised-source and workplace verification.
