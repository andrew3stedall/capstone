# Block 13 - Mixed Retrieval and Application

## Core idea

Use closed-book retrieval before applying Week 2 concepts to a new circuit representation. Keep facts, inferences, assumptions and unresolved questions visibly separate.

## Retrieval prompts

- Define normal current path, overload-like condition and short-circuit-like condition.
- Explain why conductor appearance does not establish conductor function.
- Match a hazard to a broad protective purpose without selecting a device rating.
- Name one condition that should trigger escalation.

## Evidence discipline

A strong answer states what the scenario shows, what follows reasonably, what is assumed and what requires an authorised source or qualified reviewer.

## Safety boundary

This note supports paper-based learning only. Exact requirements, ratings, operating times and installation decisions remain `reference_check_required` and `review-required`.
