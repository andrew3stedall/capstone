# Block 08 - Circuit Purpose and Load Grouping

## Core idea

Group loads by a stated functional rationale, not habit. Separate a provisional planning decision from a verified design or compliance conclusion.

## Retrieval prompts

- What is circuit purpose?
- Which evidence can justify grouping loads?
- What is a continuity consequence?
- Why can a sensible grouping remain unverified?
