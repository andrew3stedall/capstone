# Block 34 - Integrated Planning Case

## Core idea
A planning proposal is only as strong as the links among purpose, circuit division, demand assumptions, conductor variables, voltage-drop reasoning and protection coordination.

## Retrieval prompts
- Which planning decisions depend on route conditions?
- What evidence distinguishes a provisional proposal from a justified one?
- How should contradictions and missing inputs be recorded?

## Safety boundary
Do not infer compliant conductor sizes, demand factors, voltage-drop limits, device ratings or fault performance. Use current authorised sources and qualified review.
