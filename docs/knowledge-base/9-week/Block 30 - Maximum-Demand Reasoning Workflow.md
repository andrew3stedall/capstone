# Block 30 — Maximum-Demand Reasoning Workflow

## Core idea
Maximum-demand reasoning starts with scope, evidence and an authorised method. Arithmetic comes only after load categories, units and assumptions are controlled.

## Retrieval prompts
- What is the supply boundary?
- Which inputs are facts and which are assumptions?
- What authorised demand pathway applies?
- Which uncertainty most affects the result?

## Evidence habit
Maintain a load inventory and assumption register. Never import remembered diversity factors or allowances without confirming their scope and currency.

## Safety boundary
This note provides no prescribed demand values, factors, supply limits or compliant design outcome. Current authorised sources and qualified review are required.
