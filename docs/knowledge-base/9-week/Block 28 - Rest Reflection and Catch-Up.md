# Block 28 — Rest, Reflection and Catch-Up

## Core idea
Recovery should repair one genuine prerequisite gap, not become an unbounded review of everything learned so far.

## Retrieval prompts
- Which Week 4 distinction remains unstable?
- Is the gap blocking or non-blocking?
- What changed example will test the correction?
- What stop rule prevents overwork?

## Evidence habit
Use closed-book retrieval before rereading. Record one strength, one uncertainty and one deliberately deferred item.

## Safety boundary
Study recovery does not demonstrate field competence or authorise switching, isolation, testing or electrical work.
