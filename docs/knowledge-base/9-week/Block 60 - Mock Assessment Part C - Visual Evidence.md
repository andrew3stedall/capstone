# Block 60 - Mock Assessment Part C - Visual Evidence

## Recall prompt
Can I separate what an image shows from what I infer and identify the evidence needed to resolve contradictions?

## Visual-evidence chain
1. Provenance and decision question
2. Image inventory
3. Neutral observations
4. Claim mapping
5. Coverage gaps
6. Competing explanations
7. Discriminating evidence
8. Bounded conclusion

A photograph cannot establish safe state, isolation, system-wide scope or compliance by itself.
