# Safe Diagnostic Boundaries

A plausible next step is not automatically safe, authorised or within competence. Stop gates include uncertainty about safety state, authority, competence, equipment suitability, evidence validity and approved procedure.

## Retrieval prompts
- Name six diagnostic stop-gate categories.
- What evidence should be preserved before escalation?
- What belongs in an escalation brief?
- Why does a likely cause not remove a safety boundary?

## Compact model
**Proposed step → safety-state gate → authority and competence gate → evidence and procedure gate → authorised progression or stop, preserve and escalate.**

## Safety boundary
This note deliberately excludes isolation, proving, energised diagnosis, access, dismantling, testing and repair procedures.
