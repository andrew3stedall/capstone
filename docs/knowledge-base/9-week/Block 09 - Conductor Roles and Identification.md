# Block 09 - Conductor Roles and Identification

## Core idea

Determine function before relying on appearance. Treat colours, labels and position as evidence inputs, not proof, and stop when contradictions remain unresolved.

## Retrieval prompts

- How do role and identification differ?
- Why is appearance insufficient evidence?
- What makes a role hypothesis verified?
- Why must a neutral conductor not be assumed safe?
