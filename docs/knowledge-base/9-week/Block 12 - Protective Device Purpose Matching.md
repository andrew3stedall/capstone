# Block 12 - Protective Device Purpose Matching

## Core idea
Start with the hazard and required protective outcome. Match a broad protective function only after identifying the circuit, source, conductor, environment and coordination evidence still required.

## Retrieval prompts
- What hazard or abnormal condition is being addressed?
- What protective outcome is required?
- What information is missing before device selection?
- Why is a device rating not proof of suitability?

## Safety boundary
Exact device selection and verification require current authorised sources, manufacturer data, installation information and qualified review.
