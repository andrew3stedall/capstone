# Block 52 - Protection and Earthing Review

## Recall prompt
Can I trace every claimed fault path back to the source and separate demonstrated role evidence from assumptions?

## Review lens
1. Claim and abnormal condition
2. Complete path segments
3. Conductor and conductive-part role evidence
4. Protective-device coordination question
5. Possible touch-risk pathway
6. Supported, provisional, contradicted or not-assessable status
7. Bounded conclusion and technical-review questions

A complete diagram is not proof of continuity, device performance or compliance. Exact arrangements, criteria and limits remain subject to current authorised sources and qualified review.
