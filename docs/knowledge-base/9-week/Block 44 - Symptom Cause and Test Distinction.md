# Symptom, Cause and Test Distinction

Keep diagnostic categories separate: a symptom is observed or reported; a possible cause is inferred; an evidence question states what uncertainty must be reduced; a result is not yet an interpretation or conclusion.

## Retrieval prompts
- Rewrite one causal claim as a neutral symptom.
- Why should an evidence question come before selection of a test?
- What separates result, interpretation and conclusion?

## Compact model
**Symptom → possible causes → evidence question → authorised evidence → result → verified interpretation → bounded conclusion.**

## Safety boundary
No field test, instrument, value, access step or repair is authorised here.
