# Block 04 - Safe Information Boundaries and Authorised Sources

## Core idea

A learning aid can support understanding without being sufficient evidence for a compliance or safety decision. Check authority, currency, scope and context before relying on an exact claim.

## Retrieval prompts

- What distinguishes a primary source from a secondary source?
- What makes a source current and applicable?
- When should a claim be marked `reference_check_required`?
- What is the correct response when safety-critical evidence is incomplete?
