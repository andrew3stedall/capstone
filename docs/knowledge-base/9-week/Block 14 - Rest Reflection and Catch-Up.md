# Block 14 - Rest Reflection and Catch-Up

## Core idea

Repair only a prerequisite gap that would block Week 3. Schedule the rest and preserve a real recovery period.

## Catch-up gate

1. Review the error log.
2. Identify at most one blocking gap.
3. Use one short retrieval-and-correction cycle.
4. Re-test without notes.
5. Record unresolved work and stop.

## Reflection prompts

- Which Week 2 distinction is now reliable?
- Which uncertainty needs authorised-source or qualified review?
- What work can safely wait?
- What exact stop rule protected recovery?

## Safety boundary

Use paper-based review only. Do not turn catch-up into unsupervised testing or alteration of electrical equipment.
