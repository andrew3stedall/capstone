# Block 35 - Rest Reflection and Catch-Up

## Core idea
Repair one blocking planning misconception through retrieval, correction and changed-example re-testing, then stop.

## Retrieval prompts
- What makes a planning gap blocking rather than deferrable?
- Which changed input would reveal a hidden dependency?
- What is the recovery stop rule?

## Boundary
Improved recall is not technical approval. Unresolved technical matters remain subject to authorised sources and qualified review.
