# Block 18 - Touch-Voltage Risk Concepts

## Core recall
Touch-voltage reasoning requires two simultaneously accessible points, a potential difference, a possible body-contact path and an exposure-duration boundary. Numerical limits and formal definitions require current authorised verification.

## Retrieval prompts
- What are the two contact points?
- What condition could create the potential difference?
- Which protective measure limits magnitude, duration or accessibility?
- Which conclusion must remain qualitative?
