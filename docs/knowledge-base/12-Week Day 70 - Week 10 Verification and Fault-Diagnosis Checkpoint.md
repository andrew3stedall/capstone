# 12-Week Day 70 - Week 10 Verification and Fault-Diagnosis Checkpoint

## Purpose

This checkpoint integrates Week 10 evidence interpretation and systematic fault-diagnosis reasoning. The learner must preserve the distinction between observations, recorded results, interpretations, hypotheses and bounded conclusions.

## Practical application

The learner builds a checkpoint dossier containing a scenario frame, evidence ledger, dependency map, competing hypotheses, contradiction register, bounded conclusion and targeted remediation decision. All work uses supplied fictional documents and excludes practical testing or field intervention.

## Assessment relevance

The checkpoint tests whether the learner can integrate verification-purpose reasoning, evidence traceability, result plausibility, hypothesis control and safety boundaries before moving into multi-domain Capstone scenarios. Its readiness score is educational only and is not an official competency judgement.
