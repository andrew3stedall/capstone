# 12-Week Day 33 - Rest Retrieval and Formula-Selection Correction

## Purpose

Provides a bounded recovery block for retrieval, error-log correction, fatigue management and readiness checking without adding new electrical theory.

## Core concepts

Retrieval; formula-selection error; boundary error; unit error; evidence error; conclusion error; correction limit; stop condition.

## R-E-S-E-T workflow

Retrieve; examine; sort; edit from the earliest causal error; terminate after three repairs, 30 minutes or a stop condition.

## Practical application and assessment relevance

Learners redraw core models, classify errors, repair no more than three high-value errors, reopen downstream conclusions and make an honest proceed/remediate/supervise decision.
