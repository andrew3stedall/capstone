# 12-Week Day 23 - Design Current Protective-Device Rating and Conductor Capacity

## Purpose

Supports conceptual coordination among design current, protective-device rating and conductor capacity while preserving evidence and review boundaries.

## Core concepts

- **Design current:** current used as a defined design input.
- **Nominal device rating:** stated rating used to identify a device.
- **Base capacity:** authorised capacity before applicable adjustments.
- **Corrected capacity:** capacity after verified influences are applied.
- **Coordination relationship:** comparison among load, device and conductor evidence.

## R-A-T-I-N-G workflow

Record the design case; assign the protective function; trace conductor conditions; identify authorised values; normalise units and apply factors once; gate the conclusion.

## Practical application

The learner compares fictional design candidates, audits correction factors and explains why one current-rating relationship does not complete cable selection.

## Assessment relevance

Observable evidence includes value provenance, device-role separation, installation-condition tracing, single application of factors, bounded conclusions and reopening triggers.
