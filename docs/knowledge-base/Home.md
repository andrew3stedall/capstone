## Content states

- `seed` — title and relationship only
- `outline` — learning goals and module plan
- `draft-unverified` — original content drafted but not technically reviewed
- `review-required` — ready for technical review
- `technically-reviewed` — checked by a suitably qualified reviewer
- `published` — approved for product use
- `superseded` — retained for history but not current

## Important source rule

This vault maps and teaches concepts. It must not become a reproduction of copyrighted standards. Notes should use original educational structure and point to authorised source clauses for exact requirements.
