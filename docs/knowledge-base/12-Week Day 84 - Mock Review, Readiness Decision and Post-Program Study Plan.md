# 12-Week Day 84 - Mock Review, Readiness Decision and Post-Program Study Plan

## Purpose

Review the untouched integrated mock through separate performance lenses, make a bounded learning-readiness decision and create an evidence-led four-week study plan without claiming formal competency.

## Practical applications

- preserving authentic performance evidence;
- separating result, process, safety, explanation, confidence and time evidence;
- prioritising safety-critical and dependency-blocking errors;
- limiting remediation to observable targets;
- testing repairs in changed contexts;
- planning ongoing retrieval, application and recovery.

## Assessment relevance

The review converts an original mock into a learning plan. It does not reproduce official assessment material, establish an RTO result, declare competency or authorise unsupervised work.

## References and review boundary

Exact technical acceptance, official assessment conditions, competency requirements and practical authority require current authorised sources, relevant RTO instructions and qualified review. Automated content remains `review-required` and not `technically-reviewed`.
