# 12-Week Day 78 - Mock Preparation, Time Allocation and Stop-Rule Rehearsal

## Purpose

Prepare a controlled mock-assessment workflow with mapped deliverables, protected review time, explicit stop rules and progression conditions.

## Prerequisites

- [[12-Week Day 77 - Week 11 Competency Conference and Targeted Remediation]]

## Practical applications

- decomposing a brief before work begins;
- protecting review time;
- planning source and completion records;
- rehearsing phase transitions;
- defining blocked, unsafe and unverifiable stop conditions.

## Assessment relevance

The block reduces avoidable control errors before the staged mocks. It is original preparation material and does not define official assessment conditions.

## References and review boundary

Any formal timing, permitted-material or assessment rule must come from the relevant current RTO instructions. Technical claims remain subject to authorised sources and qualified review.
