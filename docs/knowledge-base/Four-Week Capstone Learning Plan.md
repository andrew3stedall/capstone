The intensive four-week sequence converts the broader Capstone Ready knowledge map into a day-by-day path for a learner who is also working full-time.

## Current sequence landmark

- [[Day 17 - Bathrooms Showers and Other Wet Areas]] develops the W-A-T-E-R workflow for identifying fixed water features, mapping provisional geometry, classifying equipment and checking location, construction, circuit and installation evidence without guessing zone dimensions or permissions.

## Practical application

Each study block combines retrieval, rule navigation, worked application and error review. Longer Saturday blocks are divided into coherent submodules so one concept can be completed and reviewed at a time.

## Assessment relevance

The sequence moves from foundational protection and navigation through design, installation, special locations, verification, fault finding and timed mock performance. It is a revision pathway, not a substitute for supervised training, RTO procedures or authorised standards access.
