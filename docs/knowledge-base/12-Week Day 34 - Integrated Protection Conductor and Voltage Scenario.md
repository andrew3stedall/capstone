# 12-Week Day 34 - Integrated Protection Conductor and Voltage Scenario

## Purpose

Integrates conductor capacity, voltage, fault-return and protection-chain reasoning through one original scenario with explicit evidence gates and reopening triggers.

## Core concepts

Integrated scenario; design gate; dependency; reopening trigger; evidence register; bounded conclusion.

## Workflow

**I-N-T-E-G-R-A-T-E** defines the boundary, traces evidence and dependencies, gates the design, reopens affected checks and limits the final claim.

## Practical application and assessment relevance

Learners produce a traceable written artefact, explain their reasoning, respond to a changed condition and limit conclusions to the available evidence. The activity supports Capstone preparation but is not an official assessment or technical approval.
