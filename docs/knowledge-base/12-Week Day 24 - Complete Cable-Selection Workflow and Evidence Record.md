# 12-Week Day 24 - Complete Cable-Selection Workflow and Evidence Record

## Purpose

Builds a complete, reviewable cable-selection evidence chain without confusing a provisional candidate with qualified approval.

## Core concepts

- **Design boundary:** defined circuit, operating case, source and route.
- **Candidate:** an option that has not passed every applicable gate.
- **Evidence record:** traceable inputs, sources, transformations and conclusions.
- **Dependency:** information required before a later claim can be supported.
- **First unsupported transition:** earliest step at which evidence no longer supports the next claim.
- **Evidence owner:** person or authorised source responsible for resolving a gap or contradiction.
- **Reopening trigger:** changed or resolved evidence requiring dependent checks to be repeated.

## Evidence labels and decision states

Inputs and claims are labelled as stated facts, derived facts, supported inferences, assumptions, contradictions or evidence gaps. Each assessment criterion is recorded as secure, developing, unsupported or `stop-required`; these are educational planning states rather than official grades.

## S-E-L-E-C-T workflow

Set the boundary; establish and label evidence; link load and protection; evaluate conductor conditions; check applicable gates; trace and transfer the bounded result. Once the first unsupported transition is reached, later suitability, operation, compliance and approval claims remain prohibited until the evidence is resolved.

## Practical application

Learners build a design-gate register, preserve competing candidates when evidence conflicts, assign evidence owners and recheck triggers, propagate at least two changed conditions and write a bounded audit summary.

## Assessment relevance

Observable evidence includes sequencing, provenance, contradiction handling, gate completeness, first-unsupported-transition logic, reopening logic, changed-context transfer, bounded conclusions and reviewer-readable documentation. Strength in one criterion cannot cancel a safety-critical or evidence failure elsewhere.

## Safety boundary

Written educational preparation only. No switching, isolation, opening, proving, tracing, measurement, testing, installation, alteration, repair, energisation, commissioning, certification or verification is authorised. Exact methods and values remain `reference_check_required`. Invented ratings, ignored contradictions, unpropagated changes and implied approval require a `stop-required` outcome.
