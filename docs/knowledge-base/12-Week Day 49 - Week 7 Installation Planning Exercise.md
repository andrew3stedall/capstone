# 12-Week Day 49 - Week 7 Installation Planning Exercise

Day 49 integrates [[12-Week Day 43 - Wiring-System Selection and Mechanical Protection]], [[12-Week Day 44 - Environmental Influences, Segregation and Support Concepts]], [[12-Week Day 45 - Consumer Mains, Submains and Final Subcircuits]], [[12-Week Day 46 - Fixed Appliances and Local Isolation Reasoning]] and [[12-Week Day 48 - Motors, Associated Protection and Control Boundaries]].

## Learning module

- [Day 49 — Week 7 Installation Planning Exercise](../learning-plans/12-week/modules/day-49-week-7-installation-planning-exercise.md)

## Core workflow

Use **P-L-A-N-I-T**: place boundaries, list conditions, apply the Week 7 workflows, note evidence and dependencies, integrate a bounded plan, then test it against a changed condition.

## Practical application

Learners prepare an original workshop-upgrade plan containing a submain, final subcircuits, fixed appliance and motor load, then revise it after an alternate source or exposed route is disclosed.

## Assessment relevance

A strong response states its design basis, maps functions and routes, uses an evidence ledger, identifies dependencies, separates supported from unresolved claims and revises every affected conclusion.

## Safety boundary

This note authorises no design approval, installation, switching, isolation, testing, alteration, energisation, commissioning, certification or verification.
