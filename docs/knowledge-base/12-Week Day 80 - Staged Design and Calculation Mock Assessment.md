# 12-Week Day 80 - Staged Design and Calculation Mock Assessment

## Purpose

Practise design-basis control, transparent calculations, interacting evidence gates and changed-input propagation in an original staged mock.

## Prerequisites

- [[12-Week Day 79 - Staged Written and Rule-Navigation Mock Assessment]]
- [[Design Basis]]
- [[Calculation Provenance]]
- [[Dependency Mapping]]

## Related concepts

- [[Input Provenance]]
- [[Reasonableness Checks]]
- [[Provisional Design Decisions]]
- [[Change Impact Analysis]]
- [[Technical Review]]

## Practical applications

- defining design boundaries;
- recording facts, assumptions and unknowns;
- showing calculation methods, units and intermediate steps;
- mapping interacting design gates;
- reopening affected work when an input changes.

## Assessment relevance

The mock provides evidence of independent design reasoning and calculation traceability. It does not approve a design or define an official assessment result.

## References and review boundary

Exact design criteria, values, tables and acceptance requirements require current authorised sources and qualified review. The fictional exercise must not be used as field instruction.
