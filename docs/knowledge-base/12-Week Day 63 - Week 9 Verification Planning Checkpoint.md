# 12-Week Day 63 - Week 9 Verification Planning Checkpoint

## Purpose

A document-based checkpoint for combining verification questions, evidence boundaries, dependencies, provenance, contradictions and bounded conclusions without providing practical instructions.

## Core model

Use **P-L-A-N-N-E-R**: Pin down the question; Locate the boundary; Allocate evidence types; Name prerequisites and dependencies; Note provenance and limitations; Examine contradictions and gaps; Record a bounded plan.

## Practical application

Create a one-page evidence plan that keeps design, inspection, result and documentary evidence separate, records dependencies and limitations, and pauses affected conclusions when identity, currency or consistency is unresolved.

## Assessment relevance

A strong response defines the question and boundary, explains dependency reasons, checks provenance, identifies contradictions and states what the evidence can and cannot support.
