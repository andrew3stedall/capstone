# 12-Week Day 39 - Accessibility Labelling and Original Defect-Recognition Scenarios

## Purpose

Develops disciplined observation and bounded defect-candidate reporting for accessibility and identification scenarios.

## Core concepts

Accessibility; identification; observation; inference; defect candidate; consequence ranking; provenance.

## Workflow

**L-A-B-E-L-S** locates evidence, annotates observations, binds them to functions, examines applicability, limits conclusions and states the required escalation.

## Practical application and assessment relevance

Learners process four original defect-recognition cards and produce observation, consequence and evidence records. The activity supports inspection reasoning but is not an official checklist or real compliance determination.
