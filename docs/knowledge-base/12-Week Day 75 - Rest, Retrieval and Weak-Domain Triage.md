# 12-Week Day 75 - Rest, Retrieval and Weak-Domain Triage

## Purpose

Protect recovery while using a short closed-note retrieval sample to identify and repair no more than three blocking, safety-critical or high-confidence weaknesses.

## Prerequisites

- [[12-Week Day 74 - Fault Diagnosis, Correction Reasoning and Re-Verification Planning]]
- [[Confidence Calibration]]
- [[Error Log]]

## Related concepts

- [[Spaced Retrieval]]
- [[Fatigue Management]]
- [[Safety Boundaries]]
- [[Assessment Readiness]]

## Practical applications

- classifying error mechanisms;
- prioritising weaknesses by consequence and dependency;
- applying minimal repair doses and changed-context checks;
- recording time, fatigue and stop conditions.

## Assessment relevance

This block supports readiness for independent timed integration. It adds no new electrical theory and does not determine competency.
