# 12-Week Day 52 - Other Special Installations and Location-Specific Controls

Day 52 extends [[12-Week Day 50 - Special-Location Method - Classify, Map Zones and Verify Sources]] beyond wet areas and compares how different dominant conditions change the required evidence and control families.

## Learning module

- [Day 52 — Other Special Installations and Location-Specific Controls](../learning-plans/12-week/modules/day-52-other-special-installations-and-location-specific-controls.md)

## Core workflow

Use **S-P-E-C-I-A-L**: state the scenario boundary, pinpoint dominant conditions, enumerate candidate controls, consult current sources, identify interactions, avoid false transfer and limit the conclusion.

- [[Wiring Rules and Design]]

## Practical application

Learners compare two original special-installation scenarios using condition, users, supply, environmental stress, candidate controls, evidence gaps and changed-condition reopening.

## Assessment relevance

A strong response explains why the installation is special, verifies the specific source, separates shared from location-specific controls and does not transfer requirements based on superficial similarity.

## Safety boundary

This note authorises no classification, design approval, site access, switching, isolation, testing, installation, alteration, energisation, commissioning, certification or verification.
