# 12-Week Day 51 - Bathrooms, Showers and Other Wet-Area Reasoning

Day 51 applies [[12-Week Day 50 - Special-Location Method - Classify, Map Zones and Verify Sources]] to wet areas without reproducing official zone diagrams or dimensions.

## Learning module

- [Day 51 — Bathrooms, Showers and Other Wet-Area Reasoning](../learning-plans/12-week/modules/day-51-bathrooms-showers-and-other-wet-area-reasoning.md)

## Core workflow

Use **W-A-T-E-R**: identify water sources, create an area map, test applicability, separate equipment and protection questions, then record and reopen conclusions after changes.

## Related concepts

- [[Safety and Electrical Risk]]

- [[12-Week Day 11 - RCD Purpose, Limitations and Interaction with Other Protection]]

## Practical application

Learners compare plan and elevation evidence, map fixed and movable water sources, review equipment position and suitability separately, and revise the analysis after a screen or hose changes.

## Assessment relevance

A strong response does not rely on room names or remembered dimensions. It identifies every exposure source, verifies current applicability, separates control layers and records unresolved evidence.

## Safety boundary

This note authorises no site measurement, classification, switching, isolation, testing, installation, alteration, energisation, certification or verification.
