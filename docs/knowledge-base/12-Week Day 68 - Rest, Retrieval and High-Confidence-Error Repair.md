# 12-Week Day 68 - Rest, Retrieval and High-Confidence-Error Repair

## Purpose

A bounded recovery block for retrieving Week 10 distinctions, identifying durable misconceptions and repairing no more than three blocking or high-confidence errors without adding new theory.

## Core model

Use **R-E-P-A-I-R**: Retrieve first; Estimate confidence; Prioritise blocking and high-confidence errors; Analyse the cause; Inspect an authorised study source; Recheck once and stop at three.

## Practical application

Complete a three-card repair sheet containing the retrieved answer, confidence rating, error cause, corrected reasoning, boundary and readiness decision.

## Assessment relevance

A strong learner can distinguish a confident misconception from ordinary uncertainty, target consequential errors and stop before fatigue turns remediation into unreliable cramming.
