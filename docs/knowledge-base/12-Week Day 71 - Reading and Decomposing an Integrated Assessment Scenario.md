# 12-Week Day 71 - Reading and Decomposing an Integrated Assessment Scenario

## Purpose

Convert a dense integrated scenario into a controlled brief of tasks, deliverables, facts, constraints, assumptions, evidence gaps, workstreams and dependencies before technical solving begins.

## Prerequisites

- [[12-Week Day 70 - Week 10 Verification and Fault-Diagnosis Checkpoint]]
- [[Evidence Quality and Applicability]]
- [[Safety Boundaries]]

## Related concepts

- [[Assessment Design]]
- [[Rule Navigation]]
- [[Dependency Mapping]]
- [[Confidence Calibration]]
- [[Fault Finding]]

## Practical applications

- extracting assessed task verbs and deliverables;
- building assumption and evidence-gap registers;
- separating design, inspection, verification, fault and documentation workstreams;
- sequencing work by critical path rather than narrative order.

## Assessment relevance

The note supports observable scenario-reading performance and reduces scope drift, hidden assumptions and premature calculation. Progression scores are educational only.
