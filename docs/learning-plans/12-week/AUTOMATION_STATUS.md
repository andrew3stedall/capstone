## Design decisions

- Day 27 removes support in controlled layers and judges success by traceable reasoning, not answer matching.
- Day 28 closes Week 4 with an independent cumulative checkpoint; critical errors override the numerical score.
- Day 29 opens Week 5 by separating calculation boundary, path convention, arithmetic and later interpretation.
- Every scenario and rubric is an original educational construct, not a substitute for standards or official assessment material.

## Review flags

- Days 27–29 remain `review-required`, `reference_check_required`, safety-critical and not `technically-reviewed`.
- Exact demand methods, conductor data, correction factors, voltage-drop equations, path conventions, criteria, device characteristics and jurisdiction-specific duties require qualified checking.
- No standards table, figure, systematic clause sequence, exact official technical value or official assessment material was reproduced.
- The modules authorise no field inspection, switching, isolation, opening, proving, measurement, testing, installation, alteration, repair, energisation, commissioning, certification or verification.
