## Design decisions

- Day 60 treats suitability as a document-supported relationship between question, context, complete-system records and limitations; it contains no operating instructions.
- Day 61 is a genuine rest and retrieval block with a 20–30 minute maximum, no new electrical theory and no more than three repairs.
- Day 62 separates a recorded result from interpretation and conclusion, and treats plausibility as an evidence-quality question rather than compliance.
- All scenarios, diagrams, workflows and rubrics are original educational models and do not reproduce official procedures, values, tables, figures or assessment material.

## Review flags

- Days 60–62 remain `review-required`, `reference_check_required`, safety-critical and not `technically-reviewed`.
- Exact equipment suitability, status, capability, verification-planning, result interpretation, acceptance and documentation requirements require qualified checking against current authorised sources.
- No standards table, official sequence, copied figure, systematic clause wording, exact value, practical procedure, official checklist or official assessment material was reproduced.
- The modules authorise no access, switching, isolation, testing, measurement, equipment operation, alteration, repair, energisation, commissioning, certification or verification.
